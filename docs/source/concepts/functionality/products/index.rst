Categorical Value Definitions
=============================

Cloud Type Values
-----------------

The ``cloud_type`` variable uses the following categorical values:

+-----+---------------------+
| Value | Description       |
+=====+=====================+
| 0   | Clear             |
+-----+---------------------+
| 1   | Probably clear    |
+-----+---------------------+
| 2   | Fog               |
+-----+---------------------+
| 3   | Water             |
+-----+---------------------+
| 4   | Supercooled water |
+-----+---------------------+
| 5   | Mixed             |
+-----+---------------------+
| 6   | Opaque ice        |
+-----+---------------------+
| 7   | Cirrus            |
+-----+---------------------+
| 8   | Overlapping       |
+-----+---------------------+
| 9   | Overshooting      |
+-----+---------------------+
| 10  | Unknown           |
+-----+---------------------+
| 11  | Dust              |
+-----+---------------------+
| 12  | Smoke             |
+-----+---------------------+
| 13  | Fire              |
+-----+---------------------+

Cloud Mask Values

+-----+-----------------+
